Floating Suction Assembly - SAFE FUEL FLOATING SUCTION™

FLOATING SUCTION ASSEMBLY - SAFE FUEL FLOATING SUCTION™ for Storage Tanks helps prevent foreign matter , water or sediment present in the stored fuel to pass on to the fuel tanks of Aircraft. This is achieved as the FLOATING SUCTION ASSEMBLY - SAFE FUEL FLOATING SUCTIONT is engineered to enable drawing of the fuel at a level nearest the surface of the Stored fuel.

Features

  • Two models available: single or double swivel joint type floating suction, according to tank size.
  • Various sizes available according to tank characteristics.
  • Materials of construction compatible with stored liquid.

Benefits

  • Reliable
  • Maintenance free
  • Long service life
