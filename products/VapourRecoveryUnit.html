Vapour Recovery Unit- CARBON TECH VRU™

Vapour Recovery Systems-Carbon Tech VRU™ find application in Storage Tank Farms & Truck, Rail & Marine loading facilities for Petroleum & Petrochemicals. The recovery process is based on adsorption on activated carbon .The activated carbon is then regenerated by Vacuum. The recovery is then made by using an available medium.
