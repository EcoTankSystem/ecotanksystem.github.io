Rim Mounted Foam Dams

Features.

  • Design allows for faster filling than conventional welded and painted systems.
  • Stainless steel and galvanized materials of construction eliminate the need for costly painting of conventional welded styles.
  • Perimeter construction means less area to fill with foam. No installation interference as with conventional foam dam systems.
  • Drain slots allow for atmospheric precipitation to drains away from the rim areas. Use of “common hardware” with primary and secondary seals.
  • Adaptable to horizontal and vertical attachment details.
  • Less chance of wind damage than conventional designs.
  • No Welding or flam cutting.
  • “In service” installation.
  • “Built –on” attachment.
  • Ease of installation.
